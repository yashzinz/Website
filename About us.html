About ifaw

IFAW is a global non-profit helping animals and people thrive together

our approach

We believe that every animal matters. Every animal is part of a species, and every species depends on its habitat for survival.

our history

IFAW was founded by Brian Davies in 1969 with the goal to stop the commercial hunt for whitecoat seals on the east coast of Canada.

our work

Together, our international programs deliver immediate and lasting impact for animals, people and the place we call home.

our impact

Since 1969, IFAW has provided hands-on assistance to and protection of animals in need, and global leadership on pressing issues impacting their health, well-being and survival.

our people

IFAW is a network of experts exploring new ideas to create real solutions.

our partners

At IFAW, we are proud to work with our partners to ensure animals and people can thrive for years to come.
